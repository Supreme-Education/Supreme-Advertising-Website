    Billboards

    We digital print custom billboard faces full color, 4 color process on a 12–13 oz flexible white vinyl. They are printed at up to 600 dpi, meaning your billboard colors will really POP. Several finishing options are available depending on how it will be mounted; our standard being a 3″ bleed and 3″ pole pocket to allow you to wrap them onto the backside of the structure and fix with poles. We use solvent UV resistant inks to reduce fading from the sun and you can expect 2–3 years life from our billboard banners before much noticeable fading.

    Giant vinyl banners are commonly used for billboards, building wraps, stadium vinyl banners and trade shows. They can be printed low resolution for billboard use or high resolution for trade show use. Production times for billboards is usually 1–2 weeks (after artwork approval).

    Please note: we print and deliver you the billboard faces for installation on your own or rented structure. We do not offer actual rental locations around the country.

    Lightboxes

    Lightboxes enhance a business’ directory and way-finding signage, monument signage and dimensional lettering and make your business easier to find at night. The wide variety of lightboxes and materials lend to eye-catching lighting effects that improve the perception of your brand.

    Because a standard lightbox is a panel with graphics applied, businesses are able to easily change the face or swap out the graphics, without a complete re-investment into a full lightbox solution — saving money over time. Lightbox faces can easily be installed to existing self-standing monument signs, pylon signs and to the building facade providing cost-effective, 24 hour advertising.

    Outdoor lightboxes are the perfect advertising and branding solutions for all types of business including: Government, manufacturing, property managers, restaurants and retail.
